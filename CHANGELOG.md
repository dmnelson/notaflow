## [0.1.0] - 2026-06-16

### Added

- Initial `notaflow` CLI for preparing NFS-e draft artifacts.
- `nfse_draft.v1` schema and validation.
- `simple-invoice.v1` importer with a compatibility alias for `local-invoices.v1`.
- Unsigned DPS XML generation through `nfse-js` plus XSD validation.
- Local portal helper, summary output, and Markdown checklist generation.
